About

OUR MISSION is to provide a single platform to all NED students and alumni, where every authenticated user can share information and content that is truly their own.

OUR PROMISE at NEDIAN BLOG is the foremost priority which remains to keep strict checks on the fake accounts and the content being regulated on NEDIAN BLOG so as to avoid misuse of information and data, cyber-bullying and invasion of privacy.

OUR ESSENCE of being students of NED University ourselves we have always acknowledged the need of being connected to our fellow peers and to those who have graduated before us; through NEDIAN BLOG we all shall remain connected with one another for all times to come despite our educational and geographical distances in order to create connections that will help us in all aspects of our lives.
